Enrollment Form

We're excited to have your child join us! Our goal is to create a positive, structured, and supportive environment where every student can grow, learn, and enjoy training. By registering for our youth program, you are agreeing to the following:

Terms

1. Commitment to the Season

Enrollment is for a 4-month season. This structure allows us to build consistency, skill development, and strong team dynamics within the group. By signing up, you are committing to the full season, regardless of attendance or changes in your availability.

2. Payment Terms

The total program fee is $595 + GST for the 4-month season.

  • Payment may be made in full at registration, or
  • Through an approved payment plan, with remaining payments processed automatically on scheduled dates

By choosing a payment plan, you authorize us to charge your selected payment method for all agreed payments.

3. Refunds & Withdrawals

All registrations are final. As your child's spot is reserved for the full season, we do not offer refunds, credits, or transfers for missed classes, withdrawals, schedule conflicts, changes in participation, or circumstances that may be outside of your control, including injury or illness.

This policy helps us:

  • Keep class sizes consistent so every student gets the best coaching experience
  • Plan the season effectively, including staffing, instructors, and facility resources

We understand that unexpected situations can arise, including injuries or skin infections, and we always prioritize the health and safety of our students. While refunds are not provided in these situations, we are committed to supporting your child's safe return to training when they are ready.

To help prevent skin infections or related issues:

  • Parents are responsible for performing regular skin checks on their child
  • Ensure proper hygiene, including showering immediately after class and wearing clean practice clothes
  • Keep cuts, scrapes, or rashes properly covered before class

We appreciate your understanding and cooperation, and we look forward to supporting your child throughout the season.

4. Attendance & Participation

Consistent attendance and active participation are key to your child's success in the program. We ask that all students:

  • Make their best effort to attend scheduled classes
  • Participate fully during training
  • Contribute positively as a teammate and training partner

We understand that occasional absences happen due to illness or exceptional circumstances, and we always aim to be reasonable and supportive in those situations.

5. Parent Support & Team Culture

Our program is built on effort, resilience, and teamwork. At times, children may feel challenged or discouraged. This is a normal and important part of the learning process. Rather than stepping away, we ask parents to support and encourage their child to continue participating and working through challenges.

If participation, effort, or behavior becomes a concern, we will communicate with you and work together to support your child. By enrolling, you agree to partner with us in addressing these situations so your child, and the team, can have the best possible experience.

6. Missed or Failed Payments

If a scheduled payment cannot be processed, we ask that you provide an updated payment method promptly. Participation may be paused until the account is brought up to date.

* By completing registration, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to these terms. We look forward to a great season together!
