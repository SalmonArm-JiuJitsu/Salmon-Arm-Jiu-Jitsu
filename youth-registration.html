Youth Enrollment

We're excited to have your child join us! By registering your child, you are agreeing to the following:

Terms

1. Season Commitment

Registration is for a full 4-month season. This helps us build consistency, develop skills, and create a strong training environment. By enrolling, you are committing to the full season, regardless of attendance or changes in your availability.

2. Attendance & Participation

Progress in jiu-jitsu comes from consistent training. Students who make jiu-jitsu a regular part of their routine develop skills and confidence faster, while also becoming dependable training partners for their teammates. To get the most out of the program, we ask that all students:

  • Attend consistently and make training a regular habit
  • Participate fully during training

We encourage students to attend as often as family schedules allow.

3. Parent Support

Like learning at school, learning jiu-jitsu isn't always easy. Every child will experience periods where training feels difficult or frustrating.


We encourage parents to support their child through those moments rather than viewing them as a sign that jiu-jitsu isn't for them. Confidence, resilience, and enjoyment are built by working through challenges.


If your child is struggling with training or motivation, we ask that you please reach out so we can help. We will also reach out if we notice any changes in participation or engagement.

4. Payment

The total program fee is $595 + GST for the 4-month season. Full payment is required at the time of registration to secure your child's spot. Spaces are limited and are filled on a first-come, first-served basis.

5. Refunds & Withdrawals

All registrations are final. As your child's spot is reserved for the full season, we do not offer refunds, credits, or transfers for missed classes, withdrawals, schedule conflicts, or injury or illness.


This allows us to keep classes consistent and plan staffing and programming properly.


If your child is unable to train due to injury or illness, we will support a safe return to training when they're ready.

6. Health & Hygiene

To help prevent skin infections and keep everyone healthy:

  • Check your child regularly for skin issues (redness, irritation, itchiness, bumps, or ring-shaped marks)
  • Ensure they shower immediately after class
  • Send them in clean training clothes each practice
  • Cover any cuts or scrapes before class
  • Ensure their nails are trimmed short prior to training
